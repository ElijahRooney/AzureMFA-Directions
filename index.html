Sign Up for

Multi Factor Authentication

In 7 Easy Steps

This process will take 2 minutes
Click the ❯ button proceed (it will appear every 10 seconds)
Click the ❮ button to go back

1 / 7 - Download the Microsoft Authenticator App
From your Android or iOS device, go to the Google Play or Apple App Store and download Microsoft Authenticator.
❮
2 / 7 - Initiate MFA Sign Up
Go to your computer and visit rescuenet.rescue.org/i/mfa.
Click the Setup Azure MFA button and sign into your rescue.org or rescue-uk.org account.
❮
3 / 7 - Obtaining Verification Code
On the Additional security verification page, select Mobile app from the How should we contact you? dropdown
Then toggle the Use verification code option. Click Set up to proceed.
❮
4 / 7 - Creating an Azure MFA Account
The Configure mobile app page will appear
From your Android or iOS device, launch the Microsoft Authenticator app, tap add Account, and then tap Work or School account.
❮
5 / 7 - Viewing One-Time Password
Use your device's camera to scan the QR code from the Configure mobile app page on your computer.
Then tap your International Rescue Committee account to view the six-digit, one-time password (generated every 30 seconds).
❮
6 / 7 - Entering One-Time Password
From your computer, enter the six-digit code from the app into the Enter the verification code from the mobile app.
Then click Verify.
❮
7 / 7 - Adding Phone Number
Finally, Add your mobile device phone number to the In case you lose access to the mobile app area, and then select Done.
❮
