Sign Up for

Multi Factor Authentication

In 8 Easy Steps

You will need your mobile device and your computer for this process
Click the ❯ button proceed (it will appear every 3 seconds)
Click the ❮ button to go back

1 / 8 - Download the Microsoft Authenticator App
From your Android or iOS device, go to the Google Play or Apple App Store and download Microsoft Authenticator. It can be downloaded and used at no cost.
❮
2 / 8 - Set up Microsoft Azure MFA
Go to your computer and visit rescuenet.rescue.org/i/mfa.
Click the Set up Microsoft Azure MFA button and sign into your rescue.org or rescue-uk.org account.
❮
3 / 8 - Get Verification Code
On the Additional security verification page, click the How should we contact you? dropdown and select Mobile app. Then toggle the Use verification code option. Click Set up to proceed.
If you prefer to receive your OTP by text message, select Authentication Phone from the drop-down.
❮
4 / 8 - Register your Account in Microsoft Authenticator
The Configure mobile app page will appear
From your Android or iOS device, launch the Microsoft Authenticator app, tap add Account, and then tap Work or School account. Do not sign into the Authenticator app!
❮
5 / 8 - Generate One-Time Password
Use your device's camera to scan the QR code from the Configure mobile app page on your computer.
Then, with your mobile device, tap your International Rescue Committee account to view the six-digit, one-time password (generated every 30 seconds).
❮
6 / 8 - Verify One-Time Password
From your computer, enter the six-digit, one-time password from the app into the Enter the verification code from the mobile app.
Then click Verify.
❮
7 / 8 - Add Backup Phone Number (Optional)
Finally, Add your phone number in the event you lose access to your app or your device. Be certain that this number can receive text messages “SMS”. Then select Done.
❮
8 / 8 - Configure your MFA Settings
You can always configure your MFA settings and accounts at any time and do not need the helpdesk support to do so!
❮
